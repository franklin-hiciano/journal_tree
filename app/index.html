how this works
Most forks don't matter. A few change everything.
Every night you're choosing what to spend the question on. Most calls — which task first, whether to skip the gym — barely move you; do them a hundred different ways and you land in roughly the same place. A few — quitting something, starting the hard thing, telling someone the truth — send you somewhere completely different. The tree's job is to help you tell those two apart, on the night they're actually in front of you.
